ENWOOD

Logo design and Identity created for a self made company “Enwood”. Branded materials included, a logo, a letterhead, a business card and design concepts. This was my first project submission in a graphic design class at the Simon Fraser University. The process taught me the basics and allowed me to get familiar with adobe illustrator and photoshop.

Overview

This project was worked on by a team of two in a span of 3 weeks. During this time, we had deliverables that needed to be submitted each week as milestones. These included company branding ideas, sketches, and draft illustrations. The main skills used for this project were brand ideation, sketching, adobe illustrator and photoshop.

Process

1 - Ideation

The first step was to brainstorm ideas for the brands name and identity. We decided to name the company Enwood, which was the combination of the names of the two teammates. We established Enwood as a high end car renting company, which also provides chauffeur and security personnel. The main target market for the company were businessmen who travel multiple destinations and regularly rent luxury vehicles.

2 - Logo Design

The logo was designed using Adobe illustrator and I used the combination of our initial sketch ideas shown above to create it. I used the outline tool to carve out just enough components of an existing picture of a car to make it appear as a supercar but keeping in mind to create a minimal and clean design. This logo stuck with the theme of luxury and supercars because of the crown added on top of the car.

The font I used was Luminari to fit the logo's theme of royalty and the first letter of the font was made to look like a trident to give a feel of empowerment. It was important to create contrast, symbol that was understandable, and represented the brand’s service. For me using black and closure method helped me create the logo and it came out perfectly detailed.

3 - Letterhead and Business Card

As final steps of the branding process, we had to design a letterhead and business card for the organization, as well as, come up with some concept ideas for the logo placement in real world objects. The designs were made using adobe illustrator and photoshop.

Reflection

This was my first attempt on a project in the graphic design field and it really helped me learn important skills of working with adobe suite softwares. This project established my confidence in visual design as it taught me different rules of displaying objects and information.
